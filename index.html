"Hello! Engineering world"

BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING
CONFERENCE
2022

An academic and inspirational celebration believing in the value of mentorship and appreciation, creating a positive change with Biomedical Engineers from over 65 countries is taking place in October, in Ghana.

2022.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@ Ghana National Theatre and Korle-Bu teaching hospital

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to the speakers from various countries about the advancement in biomedical engineering research.

  • Exhibition

    Appreciate various inventions and prototypes by various talented biomedical engineers all over the world.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic.

  • Workshop

    Try creating your own work using open source license rather than just watching.

  • Network

    Get opportunities to connect with experienced professional biomedical engineers around the world.
